USED IN README: in the "Discord notifications" section

WHAT TO CAPTURE:
A real Discord embed posted by Twitcharr when a streamer goes live. This
sells the social/notification angle.

CHECKLIST:
- Configure the Discord webhook in Twitcharr settings
- Click "Send Discord test" OR wait for an actual go-live event
- In the Discord channel, the embed should show:
    * "Twitcharr" as the bot/webhook name
    * Title: "🔴 <Streamer> ist jetzt live!"
    * Twitch-purple left border (#9146FF)
    * Description: the actual stream title
    * Two inline fields: "Spielt: <Game>" and "Zuschauer: <count>"
    * Thumbnail (top right): streamer's profile picture
    * Large image: live preview frame from the stream
    * Footer: "Twitcharr"
- Crop: just the embed message in Discord, not the whole Discord client
- Best with a real popular streamer so the preview looks good

RESOLUTION: 1024×600 PNG (Discord embeds are wider than tall)
